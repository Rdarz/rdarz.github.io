Our Profile

Freedom Ancillary Health (FAH) is a national leading healthcare provider that’s committed to exceeding the expectations of our clients, patients and service providers through ethical and professional conduct, provision with compassionate care. Based in Florida, Freedom Ancillary Health founders have over 45 years of experience within the healthcare industry. Each employee of FAH brings their expertise and unparalleled commitment in providing the highest level of care and services to our customers.

We’re a privately held organization that provides ancillary care services to all worker’s compensation and insurance companies. All patient care plan is prioritized with recommendation to ensure cost savings to the payer prior to the execution of patient care. This innovative cost-effective solution has saved our clients millions of dollars in controlling medical costs within the healthcare industry. Our pro-active approach provides the best strategy for all parties involved to ensure best patient care for optimum outcome in a timely manner.

We provide our clients with a bundle of patient services like Audiology, Clinical Lab Services, Durable Medical Equipment & Medical Supplies, Diagnostic Services, Home Health Care, and Orthotic & Prosthetic Services at competitive rates. Our network of healthcare professionals goes through an extensive credentialing process to ensure their licenses, certifications, accreditations, and COI are always active and in current status. This screening process is to ensure each patient is receiving the highest level of care and comfort from one of our healthcare providers who meets the benchmark of State, Medicare and HIPAA guidelines.
