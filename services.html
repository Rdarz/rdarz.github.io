Orthotic & Prosthetic Services

Freedom Ancillary Health understands that living with certain medical conditions and disorders or recovering from injuries and surgeries can be difficult. We do our absolute best to provide you with superior products and services. Our patients will rediscover the confidence that comes with the independence to get back into everyday living. Our nationwide Orthotic & Prosthetic provider network offers a full range of options and services. All practitioners are licensed, board certified, accredited, insured, experienced and qualified. We have a wide range of options for fabricating custom orthotics and prosthetics for patients that need support for function and comfort.

    Orthotics:
  • Upper Extremity
  • Lower Extremity
  • Spinal Orthosis
    Prosthetics:
  • Upper Extremity
  • Lower Extremity
  • Mastectomy
    Each patient will receive the following at time of service by a licensed, trained & experienced practitioner:
  • Evaluate, design, fabricate, fit and align orthosis or prosthesis
  • Examining and evaluating the patient’s needs in relation to their functional loss
  • Select materials and components
  • Make all necessary casts, measurements, model modifications and layouts
  • Perform fittings, alignments and adjustments
  • Evaluate the orthosis or prosthesis on the patient and instruct the patient in its’ use

Also, our practitioners provide and accommodate wide range of custom-molded and extra-depth shoes for numerous activity levels. These shoes are available for those who have orthopedic and athletic needs. Custom shoe inserts can be made to accommodate a variety of needs from amputations, flat-foot, to joint deformities.
