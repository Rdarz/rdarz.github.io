Privacy Policy

  • Notice

    Freedom Ancillary Health, Inc is firmly committed to protecting your privacy. You should understand what we do with the personal information that you provide to us. That's why we've created this Privacy Policy, which discloses our information gathering and dissemination practices.

  • Overview

    In general, using our website (www.freedomancillaryhealth.com) as a site visitor may be considered anonymous. We do not engage in any activity that will personally identify our site visitors.

    We may use the information you send through our website to healthcare providers that will administer services you’ve requested for patient care. If you are a customer or supplier of ours, we will also use your personal information for the purposes of providing the services or information you or your organization have requested and contacting you about other services or events that may be of interest to you and for other legitimate business purposes, as permitted by applicable laws.

    Any personal information that we have collected or stored about you, or that you have provided to us, may be transferred to a future purchaser of our company and/or its assets.

    The data collected via our site is stored in a secure server with periodic backups of such data but will not give or sell it to anyone else.

  • Registration

    Using www.freedomancillaryhealth.com doesn’t require registration of any kind other than voluntary registration for patient care services, joining the provider network, and any other correspondence you wish to have with our organization. The personal data collected for these purposes will not be sold to anyone outside of Freedom Ancillary Health and will be used by Freedom Ancillary Health for one-on-one contact with the registrants.

    Freedom Ancillary Health may collect certain information by automated means, such as patient and healthcare provider personal data which is stored on our system. For further information regarding personal data on site and why we use them, please read our Terms of Services.

    We sometimes collect anonymous information from visits to our website to help us provide better customer service. This anonymous information is used internally to ensure we have checks and balances. We may use this data to analyze trends and statistics and to help us provide you with better overall services.

  • Links

    This website may contain links to other websites. Freedom Ancillary Health is not responsible for the privacy practices or the contents of such websites, nor do we take any responsibility for the opinions of third parties expressed on or through our website.
